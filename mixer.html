How Mixing Works

Your coins enter a shared pool with hundreds of other users. The mixer breaks up amounts, routes through intermediate "peeling chain" wallets, and recombines to output at your destination — severing all blockchain links.

Blockchain Analysis Evasion

On-chain analytics firms (Chainalysis, Elliptic) use heuristic clustering to track coin flow. Tumblers introduce noise that breaks these heuristics, making tracing computationally infeasible.

Why Delay Matters

Timing correlation is the #1 attack vector. If your output arrives 2 minutes after your input, blockchain analysts can link them trivially. A 24-hour delay eliminates this.
